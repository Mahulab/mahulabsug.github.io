About Us

We are a scientific research and educational facility located at the Department of Marine and Fisheries Science at the Unniversity of Ghana, Legon. We seek to provide knowledge that serve and inform the society on complex environmental challenges.We aim at providing advancement in ocean and coastal ecosystems management and policies. We collaborate with other researchers and institutions around the globe to provide hand on educational programmes that focuses on mentorship and training of the next generation of world class scientists and researchers in the field of Marine Science.
Our work is focused on areas of marine biogechemistry where we specialize in paleoenvironmental reconstruction, marine pollution, water quality assessment, ecotoxicology and human health risk assessment of heavy metals as well as geochemical processes in nearshore marine systems

Our
Mandate

Promoting collaborative, user-inspired cross disciplinary research on the most critical concerns
facing coastal ocean system's sustainability.
Promoting science-based mangagement of coastal and ocean resources by engaging
stakeholders.
Educating a diverse and creative workforce in coastal and marine research and policy to meet
changing environmental,economic and social problems of the 21st century.

Dr. Edem Mahu

(Chief Investigator)

Senior Lecturer at the Department of Marine and Fisheries Sciences of the University of Ghana. I teach and mentor both undergraduate and postgraduate students.My research area includes;

  • Paleoenvironmental reconstruction.
  • Marine pollution.
  • Water quality assessment
  • Ecotoxicology and human health risk assessment of heavy metals.
